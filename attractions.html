Attractions

Explore the fascinating attractions that make Münster a truly remarkable city.

St. Paul's Cathedral

Visit the iconic St. Paul's Cathedral, a masterpiece of medieval architecture. Marvel at its towering Romanesque towers and discover the intricate details of its breathtaking interior.

The Peace Hall

Step into history at the Peace Hall, located inside the beautiful old city hall. This historic landmark is where the Treaty of Westphalia was signed in 1648, bringing an end to the Thirty Years' War.

Cobbled Streets

Take a stroll through the charming cobbled streets of Münster. Admire the historic buildings, quaint shops, and cozy cafes that line these picturesque streets.

Museums and Galleries

Immerse yourself in the city's vibrant art and culture scene. Explore the Museum of Art and Culture, the Civic Museum, and don't miss the Picasso Museum, home to a prestigious collection of Picasso's lithographs.

Market Square

Experience the lively atmosphere of the biweekly market held in the main square. Discover fresh local produce, arts and crafts, and immerse yourself in the vibrant energy of the city.

These are just a few highlights of the many attractions waiting to be explored in Münster. Plan your visit and create unforgettable memories in this charming city.
